Integrated Learning Approach

Owing to my 15 years teaching and research experience I have identified the gaps where students are lagging behind. Usually students study different subjects in a very disconnected manner and couldn’t relate the subjects with each-other. Since today is the time for Outcome Based Education (OBE) therefore it is essential to have an interdisciplinary approach for the topic/subject that can help us to understand the basic concept of the subjects and believe me with this approach one can easily apply their logic to solve the problems/objective questions.
